LAKME FASHION WEEK

Lakme Fashion Week is jointly organized by Lakme, the number one cosmetics and beauty services brand in India and IMG Reliance Pvt. Ltd., leader in sports, fashion and entertainment marketing and management. Lakme Fashion Week has been conceived and created with a vision to "Redefine the future of fashion and integrate India into the global fashion world." Lakme Fashion Week is organized twice every year.
